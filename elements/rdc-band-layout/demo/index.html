Redhat.com Band Layout

A band is a vertical unit of the page. Instead of a content area and sidebar that runs from the top to the bottom of the page, our pages are split up into horizontal 'bands'. Each band can have its own background and layout, and can contain anything from a single component to a complex nested layout comprised of several different components.
